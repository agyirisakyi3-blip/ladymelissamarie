Mission & Vision

Our Divine Mandate and Purpose

Mission

To bring spiritual restoration, healing, and deliverance to lives through prayer, the Word of God, and the leading of the Holy Spirit raising believers who walk in holiness, obedience, and Kingdom purpose.

Vision

To see a generation and families restored, spiritually awakened, and fully aligned with God's will living as impactful believers who reflect Christ in character, power, and purpose across the nations.

Purpose

To serve as a vessel of divine restoration and transformation helping individuals recover their identity in God, overcome spiritual and emotional bondage, and step confidently into their God-ordained destiny.

Inspiration

Inspired by a divine burden for broken lives and spiritual awakening, Lady Prophetess Melissa Marie Justine Agbelom is driven by a deep conviction to see healing flow, destinies realigned, and lives transformed through the power of God's presence and truth.
